## How to Get Featured

1. Make a contribution — code, documentation, design, or testing.
2. Open a pull request or suggest an addition via the issue tracker.
3. Once merged, your name will be added here in the next release.

We welcome contributions of all kinds. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for our contribution guidelines.
